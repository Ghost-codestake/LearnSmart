Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to common questions about our Tech school services, support, and solutions

What kind of courses do you offer?
We offer a wide range of technology-focused courses including Cybersecurity, UI/UX Design, Data Analytics, Digital Marketing, Software Development, Frontend Web Development, Product Management, and Graphics & Visual Design with AI Tools.
Do I need prior experience to enroll?
Not at all! Our courses are designed for both beginners and professionals. We start from the basics and gradually move into advanced concepts with hands-on projects.
How long does each course take to complete?
Depending on the course, the duration ranges from 8 to 16 weeks. Some advanced programs may run longer to include real-world projects and mentorship.
Are the classes online or in-person?
We provide both online and physical classes. You can choose the mode that suits your schedule and location best. Online classes are conducted live, with recorded sessions available for review.
Will I get a certificate after completing a course?
Yes. All students who complete their courses and projects successfully will receive a recognized certificate of completion, which can be added to your portfolio and LinkedIn profile.
Are there practical projects during the training?
Absolutely! Every course includes real-world projects and case studies to ensure you apply what you learn. By the end, you'll have portfolio-ready work to showcase to potential employers.
